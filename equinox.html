Feature concept build on top of the Equinox mobile app
Scenario

Equinox is a luxury gym with spas, over 100 different group fitness classes, and world-class personal trainers. Even with all of these amenities sometimes you still feel that you are not doing the best workouts to accomplish your fitness goals. However not everyone can easily afford a Equinox personl trainer on top of their membership costs to help train them. I found myself in this scenario as someone who sometimes feels lost when going to the gym and will just hit the treadmill or take the same fitness class once a week.


What if you had access to a weekly workout plan made with your specific fitness goal in mind?

Empathy
I spent some time interviewing other Equinox members to understand what details matter the most to them.
Ideating

To rapidly prototype my ideas, I cam up with feature ideas and flows that could help address my issue. I also began my prototyping process with sketched wireframes.

Solution

The solution is to create a new feature where members can get a weekly personalized workout plan tailored for their goals. The workout plan would consist of recommended Equinox classes to sign up or a workout guide using the weights or other gym equipment. Below shows the user flow for the proposed solution.

Reflection

In the future I would explore using the Furthermore Digital Magazine feature which currently has low visibility on the mobile app but includes healthy lifestyle tips, workouts, and other guides that could enhance my concept idea. I would also do research on the impact of the words I used to describe the fitness plans, ex "Trim Down" and "Get Stronger". Would users be more willing to select a fitness plan if it said "lose weight" instead? Also I would add more options to personalize the workout plan, such as allowing the user to choose if they want more classes or workout guides on their plan.

Thanks for exploring this far.
